Moderation commands
<%= prefix %>ban
Ban one or more people from teh server eg.
Usage:
<%= prefix %>ban <members> [reason]
More Info:
Just like a massban command (an aliase to this command), you can ban more than one person by seperating the names with a space. (Like in example below)
Example:
<%= prefix %>ban @user Spamming
<%= prefix %>ban @user @user2 Spamming
<%= prefix %>clear
Clear a number of messages you want from a channel or users
Usage:
<%= prefix %>clear <limit> [members]
More Info:
Clear messages from everyone in the current channel or just from one or more people
You can clear from more than one person by speratin their names/IDs/ping with a space
Examples:
<%= prefix %>clear 10
<%= prefix %>clear 10 @user
<%= prefix %>clear 10 @user @user2
<%= prefix %>kick
Kick one or more people
Usage:
<%= prefix %>kick <members> <reason>
More Info:
You can kick more than one people by seperating their names/id/pings with a space eg. (look at the example below)
'masskick' command is just an aliase to command.
Example:
<%= prefix %>kick @user Spamming
<%= prefix %>kick @user @user2 Spamming
<%= prefix %>tempban
Ban a member and (s)he'll be unbanned after the seconds given
Usage:
<%= prefix %>tempban <members> <seconds> <reason>
Example:
<%= prefix %>tempban @user 5 Spamming
<%= prefix %>tempban @user @user2 5 Spamming
<%= prefix %>timedban
Ban one or more people from the server after certain seconds
Usage:
<%= prefix %>timedban <members> <seconds> <reason>
Example:
<%= prefix %>timedban @user 5 Spamming
<%= prefix %>timedban @user @user2 5 Spamming
<%= prefix %>timedkick
Kick one or more people from the server after certain seconds
Usage:
<%= prefix %>timedkick <members> <seconds> <reason>
Example:
<%= prefix %>timedkick @user 5 Spamming
<%= prefix %>timedkick @user @user2 5 Spamming
<%= prefix %>unban
Unban one or more banned members by their ID
Usage:
<%= prefix %>unban <member ID> [reason]
Example:
<%= prefix %>unban 12345
<%= prefix %>unban 12345 98765
